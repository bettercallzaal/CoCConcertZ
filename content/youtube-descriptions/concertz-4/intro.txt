COC Concertz #4 Intro — Joseph Goats, Tom Fellenz, Stilo World

The opening segment of COC Concertz #4, where host BetterCallZaal introduces three musicians who have been building at the intersection of music and Web3 for years. All three are in active rebrand — Tom Fellenz refocusing on acoustic guitar and vineyard performances under Fellenz, Joseph Goats expanding from Jose to reach English-language audiences while staying rooted in Spanish-language music and the Huöttöja indigenous community, and Stilo World building out his artist infrastructure with vibe coding ahead of the Ambition album release.

The intro covers each artist's current trajectory, a discussion on vibe coding and the COC artist portal that was built for this show, Duo's crowdfunding milestone from COC Concertz #3, and the shared vision of using Web3 tools to create long-term sustainability for independent musicians. Stilo World talks about creating a tool for the next event, echoing how WaveWarZ pairs events with purpose-built tooling. The conversation touches on monetization, community knowledge sharing, and what it means to build as a musician when the tide is low.
