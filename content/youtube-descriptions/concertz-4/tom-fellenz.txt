COC Concertz #4 — Tom Fellenz live acoustic set

Tom Fellenz is a guitarist and composer who returned to live acoustic playing through Web3 metaverse stages. A technical failure at EasyCorner in December 2021 left him no option but to play live guitar, and performing at the Metaverse Music Festival in Decentraland in March 2022 rebuilt his technique from the ground up. He currently works at OV Systems, consults and mentors in the Web3 music space, and is building toward vineyard and live venue performances under the rebrand to Fellenz.

This set is five acoustic originals, each with a different emotional center. Setting the Scene opens the space. From Above is a piece about 20 years in the making, drawing on themes of higher connection deepened through the COVID years and personal spiritual exploration. Another Page Turned marks the moment he relearned guitar and pivoted as a musician. In Quieter Times is more contemplative, written during a period of focused personal work. A Tale of Fall is the newest piece, written in October and November, about the incubation that happens when fall gives way to winter — a tale of fall, not falling.

Tom speaks throughout about having a conversation with his guitar rather than performing at it, about the influence of spiritual community on his creative process, and about the communities that shaped his path — Meteorites, Token Smart, Third Planet. He drops alpha about no longer traveling to Europe in October, opening the possibility of a fall gathering stateside.
