COC Concertz #4 — Stilo World performs the Ambition album live

Stilo World is a Caribbean-influenced hip-hop and R&B artist based in Los Angeles who recently released his debut album Ambition. He has hosted 150+ consecutive weekly VR concerts and approaches artist readiness as core practice — preparation meeting opportunity. For this set, he presented a previously recorded live stream of the full Ambition album while jumping into VR to watch alongside the audience in the Spatial.io metaverse venue.

The performance covers nine tracks. Together is a statement of collective ambition and community identity. Hero is built around care and love for the people around him, shouting out his collaborators Mimi, Steve, and Davey. I Want You to Stay shifts into beach-side romance. Paradise moves through imagery of friendship, elevation, and arrival. Mama is a tribute. Bonita is a deep cut for his core listeners. Save Me is slower and more vulnerable. Nothing to Lose closes the set with an affirmation built on over a decade of preparation — ten years, no sleep, never called off.

Between tracks, Stilo speaks about building the tools musicians actually need before the wave arrives, using vibe coding to create his own infrastructure. He encourages artists to start thinking about monetization now, not after the moment passes. He closes by announcing his March 19th live show at Tennessee's Tavern in Hermosa Beach, Los Angeles.
