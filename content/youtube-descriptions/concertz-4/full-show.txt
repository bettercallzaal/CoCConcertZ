COC Concertz #4 ft. Joseph Goats, Tom Fellenz, Stilo World — live music from the metaverse

COC Concertz #4 brings together three musicians deep in the Web3 music space — Joseph Goats, Tom Fellenz, and Stilo World. All three are in active transition: rebranding, refocusing, and sharpening how they show up as artists and builders. Joseph Goats is a Venezuelan singer-songwriter who recently rebranded from Jose, performing original Spanish-language songs rooted in indigenous community work with the Huöttöja people of the Venezuelan Amazon. Tom Fellenz is a guitarist and composer who returned to acoustic playing through metaverse stages, now focused on vineyard performances and consulting at OV Systems. Stilo World is a Caribbean-influenced hip-hop and R&B artist based in Los Angeles who just released his debut album Ambition and has hosted 150+ consecutive weekly VR concerts.

Joseph Goats performs three songs live from Caracas on a rainy day with the Venezuelan Amazon rainforest as his backdrop — No Winters Land, Calma, and Compass (Brujula). Between songs, he shares a Giveth fundraiser for the Huöttöja community seeking $300 for a solar panel and fridge. Tom Fellenz plays five acoustic originals — Setting the Scene, From Above, Another Page Turned, In Quieter Times, and A Tale of Fall — each carrying years of personal reflection and a journey back to the guitar catalyzed by a technical failure at EasyCorner in December 2021 and the Metaverse Music Festival in March 2022. Stilo World presents the full Ambition album as a recorded live performance while watching from VR with the audience, covering Together, Hero, I Want You to Stay, Paradise, Mama, Bonita, Save Me, and Nothing to Lose.

The show runs across X Space, Twitch, TikTok, and a Spatial.io metaverse venue simultaneously, with discussion throughout touching on vibe coding, the COC artist portal, WaveWarZ benefit battles, monetization tools for independent musicians, and the broader question of how Web3 can support long-term artist sustainability. Co-hosted with Th Revolution from the Community of Communities.
