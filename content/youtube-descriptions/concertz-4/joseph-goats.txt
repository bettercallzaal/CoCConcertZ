COC Concertz #4 — Joseph Goats live from Caracas, Venezuela

Joseph Goats is a Venezuelan singer-songwriter who has been using music as a vehicle for social coordination and indigenous community support since the early days of the metaverse concert wave. Based in Caracas, he has performed across Web3 environments with Giveth and WaveWarZ as key tools in his ecosystem, approaching each performance as a form of storytelling that connects his roots in the Venezuelan Amazon to a global audience. He recently rebranded from Jose to Joseph Goats as part of an intentional move toward English-language listeners while keeping his core identity rooted in Spanish-language music and community.

This set is three original songs performed live from Caracas, with the Venezuelan Amazon rainforest visible in the background. No Winters Land is an early Web3 composition originally written for a DeFi contest about surviving harsh conditions - it placed second and helped fund an early trip to the Huöttöja community. Calma is a rainy-day piece built to shift frequency, performed here as rain falls outside. Compass, known in Spanish as Brujula, draws from the Four Agreements and treats the concept of a smart contract as an indigenous philosophical model for coordination - a bridge between his two linguistic audiences.

Between songs, Joseph shares context about the Huöttöja indigenous community currently on tour through Venezuela, with a live fundraiser on Giveth seeking $300 for a solar panel and fridge. He speaks about finding your musical note as both a mathematical and personal process, connecting harmonic theory to mission and values. He closes with a direct call to action around an April 22 Earth Day activation, a Live Earth event happening the same week, and an upcoming Giveth funding round.

Note: The indigenous community Joseph references throughout this set is the Huöttöja community. This is the correct spelling as provided by the community itself. We apologize for any incorrect spellings that may appear in captions, titles, or other materials.
