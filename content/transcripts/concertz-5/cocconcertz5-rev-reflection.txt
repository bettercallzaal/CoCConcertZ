So for you legends that may have missed it this weekend, we had the pleasure of hosting an event with The ZAO and Zaal, and we featured GodCloud. The space that we did was called "A Day in the Life of GodCloud," where we was able to delve into the life of GodCloud - what he does in a day, how he produces his music, the skill set that is acquired, some of his life's journey, his work's journey, some of the content that he makes, songs that he's made. We was able to listen to some of his music and really get to further understand who he is as an artist, where it is that he's come from and where it is that he is going.

It was an amazing space, we really enjoyed it. And it was in honour of our COC ConcertZ where the Community of Communities and The ZAO have collaborated together to put on these virtual events every month to help artists showcase their skill set, to create a memorable experience between artist and listener - so that the artists can showcase their skills through live streaming into a virtual environment, and the listeners, the fan base, the community are able to come into this virtual environment and enjoy the music from the artist, listening to them speak about their songs and get a further understanding of what the songs stand for, what they mean, the inspiration behind the creation of them.

But also get to enjoy their experience in a 3D virtual world with their friends, with people they've never met, and with the artists themselves as well. So that is the core fundamental behind the COC ConcertZ - to create a memorable experience for the listener, and also give the artists an opportunity to showcase their music in a virtual environment in a way that was never possible before. If people aren't able to put performances on or travel to these events, we can put an event online for people to connect to, anywhere and everywhere, making it more inclusive, more engaging, and more connective than ever before.

Thank you. I appreciate everyone that came over the weekend. Sorry if you missed it - it was recorded so you can go back and listen. And our next virtual environment is coming soon - stay tuned for that as well. Thank you.

- ThyRevolution
